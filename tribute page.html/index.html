The Four Emperor's of The Sea

This tribute is for the four Emperor's of the sea. We will look into each of them, and how they've gotten into the positon they're in now. From left to right we have Marshall D. Teach (blackbeard), Red Hair Shanks, Monkey D. Luffy, and Finally Buggy The Clown.

First we will look into Buggy the clown

Buggy The Clown has a history of being in the right place at the right time, and has made it to this level of power purely through luck.

He does have quite the storied past though. From being on the former pirate king Gol D. Rodger's pirate ship at a young age. As well as being former crewmates with current Emperor of the sea Red Hair Shanks. Before being named as a Emperor he was also a warlord for the world government. A few other accomplishments he has under his belt are

  • Being seen working alongside former Emperor Whitebeard
  • Fighting the worlds strongest swordsman and coming out unscathed
  • He was a part of the escape from Impel down, and had a majority of the prisoners swear their allegiance to him
  • Created Buggy's delivery which is a mercenary group for hire

Next we will look into a dastardly pirate known as Marshall D. Teach aka Blackbeard

Blackbeard is a ruthless pirate who will stop at nothing to gain the power he seeks. A former crewmember of the Whitebeard pirates he traveled with the crew putting up the act of being a part of the family

That was until another member of the crew found what he was after...the dark-dark fruit. It was at this point he killed the person who found it to take the fruit for himself. Ever since then he has traveled the seas in pursuit of absolute power. Here are some of his accomplishments along the way.

  • He defeated Fire Fist Ace, and turned him into the world government to earn the role of warlord
  • He stormed the prison Impel down gaining some of the strongest crewmembers after freeing them from the lowest levels
  • He is now in control of two of the strongest devil fruits to exist after taking Whitebeards when he died in battle
  • He is the only person known to have harm Red Hair Shanks leaving him with a scar over his eye.

Lastly we will look into our protagonist of the show, and the person who adventure we've been following Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy has had quite the adventure and has plenty of accomplishments since he became a pirate. He has an unrelenting passion to become the next King of the pirates, and will not let anyone stop him from achieving his dreams.

He has brought together an incredibly strong crew, and made plenty of powerful allies along the way that will follow him to the end of the earth. Here goes just a few of his many accomplishments.

  • He has defeated 3 warlords of the sea including Crocodile, Doflamingo, Gecko Moria
  • He fought alongside Whitebeard at marineford in attempt to save his brother gaining the respect of the former Emperor, and all of his crew
  • He has gone to the fromer Emperor Big Moms territory left alive, and then traveled to Wano to defeat the then Emperor Kaido
  • His father is known as the most wanted man and the leader of the revolutionary army Dragon
